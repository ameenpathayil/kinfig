About Kinfig

Kinfig is an exclusive social media platform that brings family emotions to one place. By enabling good communication, sharing special moments, managing family trees. Kinfig here aims to create a close connection between family members. In this rapidly moving world where families share emotions and communicate via different social media, Kinfig's on a mission to systematically centralize family's emotions, strengthen their bonds, assist them to lend a helping hand between each other, and create an extensive connection between them.
